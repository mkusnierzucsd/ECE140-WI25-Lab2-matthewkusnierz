Our Product: Self-Organizing Bookshelf

The Self-Organizing Bookshelf works in tandem with an app to sort its books to the user's preferences. From the app, the user can create and specify sorting methods such as title, genre, author, publication date, etc. Through a system of rails, arms, and cameras, the bookshelf will automatically scan the title of an inserted book, crosscheck it with a database, and determine how to sort it without any thought from the user.

Customer Interview Validation Results

We found that customers who worked in institutions like libraries or bookstores were the most enthusiastic about the idea. There were also curiosities raised by its potential at managing paper files and materials. Most customers did not consider the product necessary for home use.
